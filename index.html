    ABOUT

    Hi ! I'm Delphine, I'm 28 years old and I live in Paris. A full time engineer in Data Science, I'm also a painter on my spare time. I started acrylic painting in 2008 and abstract painting in 2020. My favorite subjects to paint are skies, trees and the sea. I also really like abstract painting as a mean of expression and I'm planning to do more of it. My creative process is all about colors and atmosphere to convey a feeling of peace and joy.
